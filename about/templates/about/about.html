About Who Killed Me

We are dedicated to creating a fair and honest gaming community by identifying, reporting, and taking action against cheaters. Our mission is to protect the integrity of online gaming for all players.

Our Mission

To build a comprehensive database of suspected cheaters and provide a platform for the gaming community to collaborate in the fight against unfair play. We believe that every player deserves an honest competitive environment.

Through advanced detection algorithms, community reporting, and transparent enforcement, we work tirelessly to maintain game integrity across all platforms.
